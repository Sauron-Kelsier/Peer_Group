About the App

Group and Peer based evaluation

Quiz IT is an android app, where all kind of students can gain knowledge freely. This is a quizing platform which provides inter and intra group discussion features to students with the teacher acting as a mediator. The role of teacher is limited only to assigning group leaders and authenticating questions that would be asked during the session. Students of a particular group collectively decide on a question and the leader sends it to all other groups. When a student gets a question, he/she will select their option and submit the answer using their tablet. The Teacher will keep a track of the students performance during this session.
